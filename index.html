📋 Short Bio

Zhenhua Tang is currently a Postdoctoral Fellow at the University of Macau under Prof. Chi-Man Pun. He received his Ph.D. from Hefei University of Technology (supervised by Prof. Richang Hong). His research focuses on computer vision, 3D human pose estimation, motion synthesis, and robotic grasp generation. He has published in AAAI, CVPR, ACM MM, TMM, TCSVT, etc.
