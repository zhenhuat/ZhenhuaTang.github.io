📋 Short Bio

Zhenhua Tang is currently a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Faculty of Science and Technology, University of Macau, under the supervision of Prof. Chi-Man Pun. He received his Ph.D. from Hefei University of Technology (HFUT) under Prof. Richang Hong, and holds a B.E. from HFUT (2015) and an M.E. from Shenzhen University (2020). His research focuses on computer vision and multimedia — including 3D human pose estimation, motion synthesis, AI choreography, and robotic grasp generation. He has published in top-tier venues such as AAAI, CVPR, ACM MM, TMM, and TCSVT.
