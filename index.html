Short Bio

Zhenhua Tang is currently a Postdoctoral Fellow at the University of Macau under Prof. Chi-Man Pun. He received his Ph.D. from Hefei University of Technology (supervised by Prof. Richang Hong). During 2023–2024, he was a visiting joint-training Ph.D. student at Singapore Management University under the supervision of Prof. Chong-Wah Ngo. His research focuses on computer vision, 3D human pose estimation, motion synthesis, and robotic grasp generation. He has published in top-tier venues including AAAI, CVPR, ACM MM, TMM, TCSVT, etc.
